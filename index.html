About Us

Progression wise we cleared every boss in wing 1 through 5.

You can expect raiding with skilled people in a lighthearted atmosphere. We maintain a small but capable roster (nobody likes to sit out and we know it!) Representing is only required when you are raiding with us.

Our primary raid days are Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday and Sunday for challenge mode. Raids begin at 20:30 CET and last for a maximum of 3 hours. Other days of the week might be designated as raid days (in case of progression or unfinished bosses).
