About Us

We are a European Guild Wars 2 guild focused exclusively on raids.

You can expect raiding with skilled people in a lighthearted and atmosphere. We maintain a small but capable roster (nobody likes to sit out and we know it!) Representing is only required when you are raiding with us.

Our primary raid days are Monday, Tuesday, and Sunday (for challenge modes) and last for a maximum of 2 hours and 30 minutes. Other days of the week might be designated as raid days (in case of progression or unfinished bosses).

Interested or got any Questions? Contact us!

If you want to join us, please understand that we only accept experienced people. Check our application sheet for the full list of requirements.
