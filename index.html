About Us

We are a European Guild Wars 2 guild focused exclusively on raids.

You can expect raiding with skilled people in a lighthearted and atmosphere. We maintain a small but capable roster (nobody likes to sit out and we know it!) Representing is only required when you are raiding with us.

Our primary raid days are Monday, Tuesday, and Sunday (for challenge modes). Raids happen between 20:30 - 23:00 CEST. Other days of the week might be designated as raid days (in case of progression or unfinished bosses).

Thus far we have defeated every single boss from wing 1 through 6 with all available achievements.

We are looking for you!

  • ...are over 21 years old
